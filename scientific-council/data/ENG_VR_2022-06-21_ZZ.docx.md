## ad 3/ Accreditation of study programmes {#ad-3-accreditation-of-study-programmes .unnumbered}

> The Vice-Rector, Prof. Zbyněk Škvor, informed the CTU Council about
> the materials submitted for the accreditation of study programmes.
>
> Intentions for accreditation of study programmes:

-   Economics and Management (MÚVS, BSP, CZ)

-   Economics and Management (MÚVS, BSP, EN)

-   Innovation Project Management (MÚVS, MSP, CZ)

-   Innovation Project Management (MFA, SME, EN) Applications for
    accreditation:

-   Theory of Building Structures and Materials (FSv, Habilitation and
    Appointment as Professor)

-   Water Management and Water Structures (FSv, Habilitation and
    Appointment as Professor)

-   Architecture and Civil Engineering (FSv, Habilitation and
    Appointment as Professor)

-   Geodesy and Cartography (FSv, Habilitation and Appointment as
    Professor)

> Result of the vote by acclamation: 45 members of the CTU SC,
>
> present and entitled to vote: 32, affirmative: 32, negative: 0,
> abstentions: 0. **The Scientific Council of CTU approved the submitted
> plans for accreditation of four study programmes implemented by the
> Faculty of Civil Engineering and applications for accreditation of
> habilitation procedures and procedures for appointment as professor in
> four fields of study implemented by the Faculty of Civil
> Engineering.**

-   **Information of the Rector doc. Petráček**

> CTU has achieved a historic success - for the first time in the
> history of measuring university quality in the prestigious QS World
> University Ranking it has reached the TOP 400. Compared to last year,
> it improved by 25 places and took 378th place out of a total of 2,642
> ranked universities.
>
> This puts it in the top 27% according to the percentile. In the
> general comparison, CTU ranks third nationally behind the UK and the
> University of Technology.
>
> The Rector further stated that CTU expects to receive Institutional
> Accreditation from the NAU in the near future. RVH has programs in the
> stack, everything is ready to start the internal accreditation
> process.
