## ad 1/ Review of the minutes of the Scientific Board meeting of 22 April 2025

No comments were made on the minutes.

## ad 2/ Proposals for appointment as professor

## 

Vice-Rector Prof. Zbyněk Škvor presented the CTU Scientific Council with
a proposal for the appointment of a professor:

(The supporting materials were posted on INFOREK.)

> [Faculty of Electrical Engineering]{.underline} **Doc. Ing. Martin
> Hromčík, Ph.D. Field: Cybernetics and Robotics**

Doc. Ing. Martin Hromčík, Ph.D. presented his professional and
scientific profile and plans for his future activities to the Scientific
Council and answered questions during the discussion.

Title of the professorial lecture:

\"Feedback and feedforward control of flexible mechanical structures\".
Result of the secret ballot: 45 members of the CTU Scientific Council,
present and eligible to vote: 30, positive: 30, negative: 0, invalid: 0.

## The proposal to appoint Doc. Ing. Martin Hromčík, Ph.D. as professor in the

> **field of Cybernetics and Robotics was approved by the Scientific
> Board of CTU.**
>
> [Faculty of Information Technology]{.underline} **Doc. RNDr. Tomáš
> Masopust, Ph.D. Field: Informatics**

Doc. RNDr. Tomáš Masopust, Ph.D. presented his professional and
scientific profile and plans for his future activities to the Scientific
Council and answered questions in the discussion.

Title of professorial lecture:

\"Verification and Control of Discrete-Event Systems\".

Result of the secret ballot: 45 members of the CTU Scientific Council,
present and eligible to vote: 30, in favour: 24, against: 2, invalid: 4.

## The proposal to appoint Doc. RNDr. Tomáš Masopust, Ph.D. as professor in the

> **field of Informatics was approved by the Scientific Board of CTU.**
>
> [Faculty of Nuclear Science and Physical Engineering]{.underline}

## Doc. Ing. Libor Šnobl, Ph.D. Field: Physics

Doc. Ing. Libor Šnobl, Ph.D. presented his professional and scientific
profile and plans for his future activities to the Scientific Council
and answered questions in the discussion.

Title of professorial lecture:

\"Symmetry, integrals of motion and magnetic fields\".

Result of secret ballot: 45 members of the CTU Scientific Board, present
and eligible to vote: 30, in favour: 30, against: 0, invalid: 0.

## The proposal to appoint Doc. Ing. Libor Šnobl, Ph.D. as professor in the field of physics was approved by the Scientific Council of CTU.

> **Ad 3/ Proposal to award the Felber Medal**

Vice-Rector Škvor informed the CTU Scientific Board of the proposal to
award the Felber Medal, 2nd class -- silver. Prof. Ing. Ondřej Přibyl,
Ph.D., Dean of the Faculty of Transportation Sciences, which submitted
the proposal, provided additional information.

(The supporting materials were posted on INFOREK).

Felber Medal, Second Class -- Silver

-   Prof. Josef Faber, DrSc. (FD)

Result of the secret ballot: 45 members of the CTU Scientific Board,

present and eligible to vote: 30, in favour: 30, against: 0, invalid: 0,
proposal for 1st class: 5.

## The proposal to award the Felber Medal, Second Class -- Silver to Prof. MUDr. Josef Faber, DrSc. was approved by the Scientific Board of CTU.

> **Ad 4/ Proposal for a member of the RVH**

Vice-Rector Prof. Zbyněk Škvor informed the CTU Scientific Council of a
proposal to appoint a member of the RVH from among the students, to be
appointed by the Rector on the recommendation of the CTU Academic
Senate:

(The supporting materials were posted on INFOREK.)

Ing. arch. Šárka Malošíková, Faculty of Architecture

## The proposal to appoint Ing. arch. Šárka Malošíková as a member of the CTU Internal Evaluation Board was discussed and approved by the CTU Scientific Board.
