ad 1/ Review of the minutes of the CoR meeting of 15 February 2022

No comments were made on the minutes.

ad 2/ Proposals for appointment as professor

The Vice-Rector Prof. Zbyněk Škvor presented the proposals for
appointment as professor to the CTU Board:

(Background materials have been posted on INFOREK.)

[Faculty of Electrical Engineering]{.underline}

doc. RNDr. Anton Trník, Ph.D.

field of study: applied physics

Doc. RNDr. Anton Trník, Ph.D. presented to the Scientific Council his
professional and scientific profile and plans of his further activities,
answered questions in the discussion.

Title of the professor\'s lecture:

\"Non-destructive measurement of Young\'s modulus\".

Result of the secret ballot: 45 members of the CTU SC,

present and entitled to vote: 38, affirmative: 12, negative: 19,
invalid: 7.

Proposal for the appointment of Assoc. RNDr. Anton Trník, Ph.D. as
professor for the field of Applied Physics was not approved by the
Scientific Council of the CTU.

[Faculty of Civil Engineering]{.underline}

doc. Ing. Vít Šmilauer, Ph.D. , DSc.

Field of study: theory of building structures and materials

Doc. Ing. Vít Šmilauer, Ph.D., DSc. presented to the Scientific Council
his professional and scientific profile and plans of his further
activities, answered questions in the discussion.

Title of the professor\'s lecture:

\"Crack-resistant binders for durable concrete structures\".

Result of the secret ballot: 39 members of the CTU SC,

present and entitled to vote: 39, affirmative: 39, negative: 0, invalid:
0.

Proposal for the appointment of Assoc. Ing. Vít Šmilauer, Ph.D., DSc. as
professor in the field of Theory of Building Structures and Materials
was approved by the Scientific Council of CTU.

ad 3/ Miscellaneous

-   At the request of the rector doc. Petráček\'s request, a short
    introduction of the new and existing members of the CoR took place.

-   All full members of the CoR have received appointment decrees for
    the period from 1 March 2022 to 28 February 2026.

-   Rector doc. Petráček will prepare a short presentation about CTU
    including the ranking for the next regular meeting of the SC, he
    will give information about CTU in comparison with other
    universities in the Czech Republic. He also informed about the
    success of the S.A.W.E.R. system, which was developed at UCEEB and
    received the 2nd place at the World EXPO in Dubai.
