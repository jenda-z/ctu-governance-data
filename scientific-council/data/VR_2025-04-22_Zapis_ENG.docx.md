> **Ad 5/ Miscellaneous**

-   Annual Activity Report 2024

> (Background materials have been posted on INFOREK.)

## The Annual Activity Report for 2024 was discussed by the Scientific Council of CTU.

-   Pror. Shkvor informed about the upcoming Assessment 2025. The MEP
    convened a meeting of MEP secretaries on 24 April 2025. The conflict
    of holding the regular meeting of the CoR on 24 June was addressed,
    as the evaluation process in the presence of foreign auditors is
    taking place this week. Rector Petracek said that if there was
    nothing urgent to discuss, only

> the morning ceremony would be held in Bethlehem Chapel at 9.30 am
> (graduation of PhD students, decrees to associate professors,
> presentation of medals).

-   The Rector also informed about his visit to the Minister of Defence,
    and described the involvement of CTU and other technical
    universities in increasing the defence capability of the Czech
    Republic in a number of areas. He also mentioned the MoD + IBM +
    university consortium.

-   CoR meetings in the 2nd half of 2025: **21 October, 18 November**
    (also a morning festive meeting in Bethlehem Chapel) and **16
    December 2025**.
