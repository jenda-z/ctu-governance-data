## OP HOW

> Rector doc. Petráček informed about 4 submitted projects of CTU within
> the framework of OP JAK - Top Research Call (aviation - FS, artificial
> intelligence - CIIRC, robotics - CIIRC, quantum technologies - FJFI).
> The minimum amount of the project was 50 mil. CTU will now follow a
> turbulent evaluation process as the overhang is enormous.

## Habilitation proceedings (documents posted on Inforek)

> Prof. Ripka pointed out that one of the proposals for the habilitation
> procedure did not contain sufficient data, i.e. it did not include a
> list of literature and the candidate\'s own publications. The
> criticism was not about the actual level of the habilitation, but
> about the posted materials.
