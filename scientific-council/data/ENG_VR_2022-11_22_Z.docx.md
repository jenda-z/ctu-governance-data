supplemented by Prof. Čuba, Dean of the Faculty of Nuclear and Physical

> **ad 5/ Miscellaneous**

-   **OP HOW**

Rector doc. Petráček informed about the upcoming projects of CTU within
the framework of the OP JAK - Top Research Call. OP JAK will help to
maintain successful teams of \"big projects\" (aviation - FS, artificial
intelligence - CIIRC, robotics - CIIRC, quantum technologies - FJFI. The
anticipated start date is autumn 2023.

-   **International advisory board**

Rector doc. Petráček and Prof. Škvor informed about the second IAB
meeting. This time the Committee dealt with pedagogy. It was recommended
that each foreign student should have a Czech \"buddy\" who would be
helpful to him/her in the social sphere. In addition, it would be
advisable for him/her to be in closer contact with one academic staff
member. This would solve better integration of foreign students among
Czech students. Prof. Matas said that the MFA should be pressured to
speed up the granting of visas. The rector

confirmed that of course the rectors of universities are working on
this, but diplomatic procedures work differently.
