## Ad 2/ Proposals for appointment as Professor

The Vice-Rector, Prof. Zbyněk Škvor presented to the CUT SC the
proposals for appointment as professor:

(Background materials have been posted on INFOREK.)

> [Faculty of Electrical Engineering]{.underline}

## doc. Mgr. Ondřej CHUM, Ph.D.

> **Field of study: technical cybernetics**

Doc. Mgr. Ondřej CHUM, Ph.D. presented to the Scientific Council his
professional and scientific profile and plans for his further
activities, answered questions in the discussion.

Title of the professor\'s lecture:

## \"Searching for images in large databases Large-Scale Image Retrieval\".

Result of the secret ballot: 39 members of the CTU SC,

present and entitled to vote: 33, affirmative: 33, negative: 0, invalid:
0.

## Proposal for the appointment of Doc. Mgr. Ondřej Chum, Ph.D. as Professor for the field of technical cybernetics was approved by the Scientific Council of CTU.

> [Faculty of Nuclear and Physical Engineering]{.underline}

## doc. Ing. Tomáš TROJEK, Ph.D.

> **Field of study: applied physics**

Doc. Ing. Tomáš Trojek, Ph.D. presented to the Scientific Council his
professional and scientific profile and plans of his further activities,
answered questions in the discussion.

Title of the professor\'s lecture:

## \"The road to 3D X-ray fluorescence analysis\".

Result of the secret ballot: 39 members of the CTU SC,

> present and entitled to vote: 34, affirmative: 34, negative: 0,
> invalid: 0. **Proposal for the appointment of Doc. Ing. Tomáš Trojek,
> Ph.D. as Professor of Applied Physics was approved by the Scientific
> Council of CTU.**

## Ad 3/ Application for a waiver of appointment as Associate Professor as a prerequisite for initiating the procedure for appointment as Professor

## 

The Vice-Rector, Prof. Zbyněk Škvor informed the CTU SC of the proposal
to waive the appointment as Associate Professor as a prerequisite for
initiating the procedure for appointment as Professor.

(Background materials have been posted on INFOREK.)

> [Faculty of Electrical Engineering]{.underline}

## Dr. Ing. Josef Šivic

The proposal was submitted by the Dean of the Faculty of Electrical
Engineering, Prof. Páta, with reference to Section 74(1) of Act No.
111/1998 Coll. After a wider discussion, the vote was taken.

Result of the secret ballot: 39 members of the CTU SC, present and
entitled to vote: 34, yes: 24, no: 7, invalid: 3

## Proposal to grant an exemption to Dr. Ing. Josef Šivic in the procedure for appointment as a professor - waiver of the previous appointment as associate professor as a prerequisite for initiating the procedure for appointment as a Professor was approved by the Scientific Council of the CTU.

## Ad 5/ Miscellaneous

-   János A. Bergou, Chairman of the International Advisory Board,
    > addressed the meeting. He spoke briefly about the evaluation
    > process and praised CTU as an excellent university. He also
    > expressed his pleasure that CTU is already working on some of the
    > many recommendations that were made in the final MEP report,
    > especially in the area of doctoral studies.

-   Vice-Rector Prof. Škvor asked the members of the Rector\'s Council
    > to nominate members of SC for the Rector\'s Awards 2020 evaluation
    > committee by 23 November 2021 at the latest.
