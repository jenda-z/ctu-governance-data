## ad 2/ Proposals for appointment as professor

The Vice-Rector, Prof. Zbyněk Škvor, presented the proposals for
appointment

as professor to the CTU Council:

(Supporting documents have been posted on INFOREK.)

> [Faculty of Architecture]{.underline}

## doc. Ing. arch. Michal SEDLÁČEK

> **Field of study: architecture**

Doc. Ing. arch. Michal Sedláček presented to the Scientific Council his
professional and scientific profile and plans for his further
activities, answered questions in the discussion.

Title of the professor\'s lecture:

## \"Method of selection and major decisions in large-scale projects\".

Result of the secret ballot: 39 members of the CTU SC, present and
eligible to vote: 31, yes: 24, no: 2, void: 5.

## The proposal for the appointment of Doc. Ing. arch. Michal Sedláček as

> **Professor of Architecture was approved by the CTU Scientific
> Council.**
>
> [Faculty of Electrical Engineering]{.underline}

## doc. Ing. Jiří JAKOVENKO, Ph.D.

> **Field of study: electronics and medical technology**

Doc. Ing. Jiří Jakovenko, Ph.D., presented to the Scientific Council his
professional and scientific profile and plans of his further activities,
answered questions in the discussion.

Title of the professor\'s lecture:

## \"New methods in the design of integrated structures and circuits\".

Result of the secret ballot: 39 members of the CTU SC,

present and eligible to vote: 31, yes: 30, no: 0, void: 1.

## The proposal for the appointment of Doc. Ing. Jiří Jakovenko, Ph.D., as Professor in the field of Electronics and Medical Technology was approved by the CTU Scientific Council.
